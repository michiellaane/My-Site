From idea to validated concept

Four focused phases. Each has a clear output you own. You decide at every step whether to continue.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    Map your assumptions, users, and the competitive landscape. Clarify what you know — and more importantly, what you don't.

    Output Research brief
  2. 02

    Conceptualisation

    Turn insights into a defined concept. Nail the value proposition, target user, and core interaction before any design work begins.

    Output Concept document
  3. 03

    Prototype

    Build a testable artefact — just enough fidelity to generate real reactions. Fast, cheap, and deliberately disposable.

    Output Clickable prototype
  4. 04

    Validation

    Put the prototype in front of real users. Collect evidence that confirms or redirects the concept before a line of production code is written.

    Output Validation report

Who this is for

  • Established companies with a new idea

  • IT consultancies without design capacity
